Stronghold Clicker

This is an incremental/clicker game.

Players buy upgrades to receive more currency per click and increase coins per second. Currency is also obtained when the game is turned off, comparing the date from the last session to the current one. There are also achievements and two mini-games. For participating in these mini-games, users are rewarded with a special currency that acts as a multiplier of the base currency per click and currency per second. The upgrade costs gradually increase until the user has to perform an "prestige" which resets all unlocked upgrades and base currency to zero, but rewards another special currency which also acts as a revenue multiplier. Users can also buy items with this currency. 

A new addition is the "Army" tab, where you can recruit soldiers, expand military buildings, send them on expeditions, and a conquest mode will be added soon.
