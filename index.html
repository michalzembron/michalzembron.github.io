About 

Hi !
My name is Michał Zembroń and I have always loved games and I was interested in how they were made.
As soon as I started learning game development from scratch, I noticed how much fun it gives me and I focused on this, although during my studies I did various projects in many programming languages ​​- larger projects mainly based on Python and Java (including Android Studio ), but also got to know C and C++. Currently, I create games based on the Unity engine in C#, although as part of my master's thesis I also worked in the following engines: Godot (GDScript), Unreal Engine (Blueprints system) and Flax (C#).

I like challenges, the last effect of which is the recent completion of my first Android game in the Unity engine - Stronghold Clicker.

I graduated from engineering and master's studies at the University of Zielona Góra in the field of Computer Science.

This page is my portfolio, in which I will post all kinds of projects I have done, I hope they will get better as I get to know the Unity engine.
