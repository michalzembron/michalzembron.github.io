Benchmark

Comparison of the performance of popular game engines.

Objective of the work

The aim of the work was to create a tool capable of measuring the performance of selected game engines (Unreal Engine, Unity, Godot and Flax). Then conduct performance measurement studies using the created test, as well as conduct a qualitative survey to identify the best-performing engine with the best graphics quality according to the respondents.
The aim of this work was to help in choosing a game engine for both development studios (especially smaller ones) and individuals who want to start creating games by comparing performance, graphics quality, and the ease of use and learning of a given tool.
The scope of the work included:
- Become familiar with the following game engines and performance measurement tools:
    - Unreal Engine 4,
    - Unity,
    - Godot,
    - Flax,
    - HWiNFO,
    - Nvidia FrameView.
- Collection of materials and literature on the above-mentioned tools.
- Create a performance test on one of your engines, and then replicate it faithfully on the other engines.
- Conducting quantitative research consisting in measuring the performance in selected engines using the test created.
- Performing qualitative research by conducting an appropriate number of questionnaires.
- Drawing conclusions based on the collected and analyzed data.
